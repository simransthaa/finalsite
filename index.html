Luxiem




On 20 December, Ichikara Inc. announced the official the fourth wave, Luxiem, started their activities with a special debut program hosted by Elira Pendora and Oliver Evans. This is NIJISANJI EN's first all-male group. Luxiem, the branch's first all-male group, saw explosive popularity ever since their debut. They are considered as a proof against a widely-held belief than there's little audience for English-speaking male VTubers.
